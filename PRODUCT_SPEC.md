# KSRS Product Specification

## Vision

A Korean learning app that teaches you what matters first. Unlike traditional flashcard apps that serve cards in arbitrary order, KSRS prioritizes high-frequency phrases, practical patterns, and real-world utility.

## Core Principles

1. **Usefulness First** - High-utility content surfaces before obscure vocabulary
2. **Patterns Over Words** - Grammar patterns that generate many sentences beat isolated words
3. **Proven SRS** - SM-2 algorithm with research-backed intervals
4. **Low Friction** - Get reviewing in < 3 seconds from app open

---

## User Personas

### Beginner Learner (Primary)
- Knows Hangul basics, wants practical phrases
- Goal: Travel conversations, basic interactions
- Pain: Traditional apps teach useless words early

### Intermediate Learner
- Understands basic grammar, building vocabulary
- Goal: Natural conversations, media comprehension
- Pain: Plateau in progress, needs structured review

### Self-Study Power User
- Creates own cards from dramas/content
- Goal: Custom vocabulary acquisition
- Pain: Existing apps lack good Korean support
